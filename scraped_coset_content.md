# Scraped Content: CoSET Nigeria (Uwem Nnyin)

This document contains a consolidation of content scraped and researched from the official CoSET Nigeria website (cosetng.org).

## 1. Organization Overview

**Entity Name:** Coalition for Socio-Ecological Transformation (CoSET)
**Local Name:** Uwem Nnyin (Efik/Ibibio for "Our Lives")
**Established:** 2018
**Location:** Marrakesh Street, Wuse 2, Abuja, Nigeria

### Mission Statement
At CoSET, our mission is to drive socio-ecological transformation in Nigeria by advocating for sustainable practices, promoting social and environmental justice, and empowering communities to create a better future for all.

### Core Philosophy
The coalition challenges the current "growth-centered" economic model in favor of one that prioritize social well-being, environmental integrity, and sustainability. They argue that the total well-being of people, society, and the environment are inextricably linked.

## 2. Key Position Papers & Publications

### A. Divestment of the Nigerian Economy
**Title:** Overview of Divestment of the Nigerian Economy for Ecological and Environmental Sustainability Position Paper
**Focus:** Advocates for shifting the Nigerian economy away from fossil fuel dependency toward environmental sustainability. It explores the divestment pathways necessary for ecological repair and social justice.

### B. Climate-Induced Loss and Damage
**Title:** Overview of Climate-Induced Loss and Damage Position Paper
**Focus:** Highlights the urgent need to address the damage caused by climatic volatility in Nigeria. It calls for institutional frameworks to handle migration, displacement, and livelihood loss.

### C. Just Transition Frameworks
**Title:** Empowering Communities Through Inclusive & Just Transition In Osun State
**Focus:** A case-study based analysis of how communities in Osun State can transition to sustainable models while protecting labor rights and local economies.

**Title:** Including Trade Unions in CoSET: A Path to Inclusive and Sustainable Transformation
**Focus:** Emphasizes the role of trade unions as critical partners in environmental advocacy and ensuring that workers are not left behind in the green transition.

### D. Governance & Policy
**Title:** Insights from the National Council on Climate Change Workshop
**Focus:** Reports on collaboration with government bodies to promote policy signals that support community-led climate action.

**Title:** CoSET’s Post-COP28 Reflection
**Focus:** A technical and social reflection on global climate negotiations, charting a specific course for environmental justice within the unique Nigerian context.

## 5. FAQs & Common Insights

*   **What is CoSET?** A network of citizens, NGOs, and journalists established to re-connect nature and society through sustainable transformation.
*   **How does it work?** Through evidence-based advocacy, policy papers, and community empowerment workshops.
*   **What is the "Growth Model" critique?** CoSET argues that defining development solely through GDP growth ignores ecological limits and entrench social inequalities.
