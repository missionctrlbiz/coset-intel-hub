# Coset Intelligence Hub - Design Plan ## Overview A modern, data-driven hub for reports and intelligence related to socio-ecological transformation in Nigeria. ## Proposed Screens 1. **Landing Page (Hub Home):** - Hero section with search. - Featured reports carousel. - Latest reports grid. - Categories/Tags filter sidebar. - Impact metrics (Active sites, members). 2. **Report Listing (All Reports):** - Advanced filtering (date, category, region). - List and Grid view toggles. - Pagination/Load more. 3. **Individual Report Page:** - Report summary and metadata. - Download/Share buttons. - Interactive data visualizations (charts/maps). - Related reports section. 4. **Blog/Intelligence Feed:** - List of recent posts (Planet Pulse). - Newsletter signup. ## Visual Direction - **Modern & Sleek:** High use of whitespace, subtle shadows, and crisp typography. - **Brand Aligned:** Leveraging Coset's teal and orange accents against a clean light background. - **Responsive:** Desktop-first design as requested.
